ABOUT

Known As The Way was founded in SE Asia with the goal of reaching people with the Gospel through social justice, discipleship, and church planting.

Our hopes and dreams for The Way, is that we would live our lives so in line with the gospel of Jesus Christ while being led by the power of the Holy Spirit, that we would look like one big family that everyone wants to be apart of (John 13:34-35). In this big family we would continue to follow Christ great commandments, Loving God and loving our neighbors (Matt 22:36-40).

Gospel

God's story



The gospel is that God himself has come to rescue and restore all things in and through the work of Jesus Christ for His Glory.

Family

Who we are



We are the children of God who live and care for each other as a family.


Way

How we live



We hope to live in such a way that others would know who God is through us.









Note: Known As The Way is in no way affiliated, nor part of, nor out of The Way International. We felt we needed to clarify this for anyone interested in knowing more about us due to the confusion, heresy and cult behavior of The Way International. Please read our statements of faith and doctrinal beliefs if you have any questions as to who we are, or where we stand on issues.






Known As The Way belongs to the Acts 29 Network, a peer-to-peer association of churches around the world called and committed to the Gospel through church planting. Acts 29 is not a denomination, but a network of like-minded pastors who are dedicated to planting local, missional, Christ-centered communities to effectively reach the next generation. Acts 29 is also a network of pastors from around the nation and world whose dream is to help qualified leaders called by God to plant new churches and replant declining churches.
